Quiz Rules

  • Each quiz consists of multiple-choice questions.
  • You have 30 seconds to answer each question.
  • Choose the correct answer by clicking on the button.
  • Your score will be calculated based on the number of correct answers.
  • At the end of the quiz, you can view your score and the leaderboard.
  • You can restart the quiz anytime or navigate back to the home page.
